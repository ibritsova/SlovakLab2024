Beginner

  • Course is designed for individuals with little to no prior experience in the Slovak language
  • Participants will learn basic Slovak vocabulary, essential grammatical structures, everyday phrases for common situations, proper pronunciation, and the construction of simple sentences.
  • Interactive lessons, encouraging active participation through conversational practice and role-playing. Multimedia resources will be utilized to enhance learning.
  • All sessions will be recorded, allowing students to review materials.
  • The course is led by an experienced Ukrainian teacher only.
  • The course runs for one year, with classes scheduled twice a week.

Intermediate

  • For learners who have a foundational understanding of the Slovak language and wish to expand their skills.
  • Students will learn more advanced grammar, increase their vocabulary, practice speaking in everyday situations, and learn about Slovak culture.
  • The course includes discussions, group activities, and real-life scenarios to help students use the language more effectively.
  • All sessions will be recorded, allowing students to review materials.
  • The course is led by an experienced Ukrainian teacher, and from the middle of the course, course will begin to include lessons with native speaker.
  • Classes are held twice a week for one year.

Advanced

  • For students who already speak Slovak well and want to improve further.
  • Students will learn advanced grammar, idioms, and vocabulary. The course will also include discussions about culture and current events.
  • Classes will involve discussions, presentations, and analysis of texts.
  • All sessions will be recorded, allowing students to review materials.
  • The course is led by an experienced Ukrainian teacher and a native Slovak speaker.
  • Classes are held twice a week for one year.

Speaking Club

  • For students who already speak Slovak well and want to improve further.
  • Participants will engage in conversations on various topics, practice vocabulary, play games, and discuss popular movies and series. They will also learn everyday language used in daily conversations.
  • The club uses interactive discussions and real-life scenarios to promote speaking confidence.
  • All sessions will be recorded, allowing students to review materials.
  • The course is led by a native Slovak speaker.
  • Classes are held twice a week.
